Biography

My latest textbook on Machine Learning fundamentals

Dr. Jonathan Hartwell is a tenured Professor of Computer Science with over 15 years of academic and industry experience in Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), and Data Science. He currently lectures at the University of Cape Coast, where he also leads the AI & Intelligent Systems Research Lab.

Prior to joining our university in 2010, he worked as a research scientist at Google AI, where he contributed to several groundbreaking projects in natural language processing.

Education

Professor Jonathan Hartwell received his Ph.D. in Computer Science from Stanford University in 2005. With a Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Cambridge, Dr. Hartwell’s research spans deep learning architectures, natural language processing, computer vision, and ethical AI deployment. His groundbreaking work in neural network optimization and explainable AI has been published in top-tier journals such as IEEE Transactions on Neural Networks and Nature Machine Intelligence.

Teaching Philosophy

My approach to teaching emphasizes hands-on learning, real-world applications, and fostering critical thinking skills. I believe students learn best when they can connect theoretical concepts to practical problems.

In all my courses, I incorporate current research findings and industry practices to prepare students for careers in technology.
